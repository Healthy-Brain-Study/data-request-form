Download Data Request

Download your data request PDF here. Check if all the details are correct. You can go back to the previous steps and generate a new PDF if you leave this page open.

After downloading the PDF please click 'Send Mail' to send an email to 'hbs-data@radboudumc.nl', don't forget to attach the downloaded PDF.
If your application is part of a larger study that has been reviewed by a scientific review committee, please enclose a copy of the letter of approval in the mail.
