Further specifications

Do you request all participants or a subset (e.g., only female participants)?


Do you request cross-sectional data (assessment 1 only) or repeated measures (all 3 assessments)?


Are there any other conditions to your data request (e.g., you only want the MRI data from participants who also filled in a specific questionnaire)?


If you request data from multiple questionnaires, measures, or tasks, it may be relevant to your project that the participants fulfill certain criteria.


To illustrate, here are examples of the options you can choose:

  1. Include all participants with at least one of the requested measurements completed, for at least one of the three assessments. This option will result in the largest sample, but also the largest proportion of missing data.
  2. Include only participants with all three assessments of all requested measurements complete. This will yield the smallest sample size, but the least missing values. Depending on the number of questionnaires you request and the type of analyses you plan, this option may be too strict.
  3. Any other option in between these extremes. For example, for each of the three assessments, include only participants who completed the task xy, plus any of the requested questionnaires they completed for the corresponding assessment.

Please think about this carefully and let us know if you have any questions about this.
