Provide data selection requirements

Construct: What does it measure? Please indicate here what construct is measured. For example if you would want to investigate mental health, indicate which of the selected measures you use as a measure of mental health.
Hypothesis: For which hypothesis (number) is the measure needed?
Outcome measure: Is the selected measure a primary or a secondary outcome measure?
Required: Which measures are absolutely required? If you select 'Yes' in the required dropdown, only participants who have these data will be provided.
Assessment: Measures from which assessment are required? For each of the three assessments only the available assessments for that measure have a checkbox.
